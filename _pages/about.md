I am an ICT and Data Science researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Big Data analytics and Natural Language Processing.
My research focuses on smart city applications, sustainable mobility, air quality monitoring, and intelligent routing solutions.
I actively participate in national and European research projects, contributing as a task leader and work package coordinator, bridging cutting-edge AI research with real-world urban challenges.

Research Interests
------
- **Large Language Models for Event Extraction**: Applying LLM-based techniques to extract and structure events from textual data, with a focus on crime news and urban events from Italian documents
- **Time Series Analysis and Forecasting**: Leveraging advanced techniques including foundation models (TimesFM, Granite) for predicting air quality, traffic patterns, and urban mobility
- **Air Quality Monitoring**: Sensor data analytics, anomaly detection, low-cost sensor calibration, and predictive modeling for urban air pollution
- **Data-Driven Applications for Smart Cities**: Multi-objective routing optimization, intelligent mobility solutions, and data platforms for urban decision support systems
