**Timeline:** November 2018 – April 2021  
**Role:** Research Fellow & Task Leader  
**Funding:** INEA CEF-TELECOM [AGREEMENT No INEA/CEF/ICT/A2017/1566782] (€2M+)

The TRAFAIR project aimed to improve air quality by understanding and predicting traffic flows in urban environments. As Task Leader for Task 1.2 (Development of tools for automatic/semi-automatic population of a unified input dataset) and Task 6.2 (Web Platform), I coordinated the release of multiple deliverables [RT1-9].

I developed a **Smart City Data Platform** using PostgreSQL with PostGIS and Timescale extensions for storing spatial and time-series data (435 GB of data for Modena alone). The platform was successfully implemented in three cities: Modena (Italy), Santiago de Compostela (Spain), and Zaragoza (Spain). I also contributed to creating the SenseBoard dashboard for monitoring air quality sensors and the project website.

The project received the **"Premio PA sostenibile"** award at Forum PA 2019 as one of 100 projects to achieve the UN 2030 Agenda goals.
